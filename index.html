WHAT IS AMETAVERSE?

PROBLEMS

In order for the huge virtual reality METAVERSE to connect with global players and run smoothly, according to the current technical analysis, whether it is the performance of hardware equipment, power supply, network speed, player comfort, etc. can not meet the requirements, the goal still remains distant. Therefore, it is possibly that in the lifetime of our generation still construct a mooted METAVERSE world.

SOLUTIONS

AMETAVERSE is a web 3 multiplayer platform powered by $AMETA. Build a metaverse on existing devices and make participants to generate ongoing income. Imagine you are playing arcade game The King of Fighters, the tokens put in by the opponent you defeated are no longer collected by the operator, but become your direct income. In addition to improving the efficiency and revenue of tokens, and also will not waste the time cost of the winner. Achieving the results of real-world ongoing income from playing games in the Metaverse.

AMETAVERSE has released 2 games that allow 4 players battle. In order to cater to participants with different preferences, the first milestone is to build no less than 30 web 3 multiplayer games in the next 3 years. Frequently Asked Questions

AMETAVERSE is a web3 multiplayer game platform that focuses on developing gambling-type blockchain games for players around the world to play.
Innovative BLOCKCHAIN GAME MODE - The winner does not win tokens from the pot or the house, but from other losers in the same game. AMETAVERSE is not involved.

Different from general gambling game platforms, AMETAVERSE does not provide classic gambling games such as POKER, Roulette, Blackjack, etc....
We will provide games such as chess, racing, strategy, fighting, leisure and other types of games,
The team is working hard to develop different types of blockchain games.

$AMETA is the utilitytoken of the AMETAVERSE and its purpose is as a betting medium when participating in multiplayer games

AMETAVERSE provides a swap system, which can be exchanged with MATIC and $AMETA,
$AMETA is linked to MATIC, and the exchange rate is 1 AMETA = 1 MATIC

Players need to exchange MATIC for $AMETA, select the game they want to play and insert the corresponding amount of tokens. After the game is over, the winner will get all the bets in the game after deducting the platform service fee. When leaving the AMETAVERSE, the wallet can continue to hold $AMETA or SWAP back to MATIC.
