Frequently Asked Questions

AMETAVERSE is a web3 multiplayer game platform that focuses on developing gambling-type blockchain games for players around the world to play.
Innovative BLOCKCHAIN GAME MODE - The winner does not win tokens from the pot or the house, but from other losers in the same game. AMETAVERSE is not involved.

Different from general gambling game platforms, AMETAVERSE does not provide classic gambling games such as POKER, Roulette, Blackjack, etc....
We will provide games such as chess, racing, strategy, fighting, leisure and other types of games,
The team is working hard to develop different types of blockchain games.

$AMETA is the utilitytoken of the AMETAVERSE and its purpose is as a betting medium when participating in multiplayer games

AMETAVERSE provides a swap system, which can be exchanged with MATIC and $AMETA,
$AMETA is linked to MATIC, and the exchange rate is 1 AMETA = 1 MATIC

Players need to exchange MATIC for $AMETA, select the game they want to play and insert the corresponding amount of tokens. After the game is over, the winner will get all the bets in the game after deducting the platform service fee. When leaving the AMETAVERSE, the wallet can continue to hold $AMETA or SWAP back to MATIC.
