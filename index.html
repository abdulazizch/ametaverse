$Ameta Features

Build on Polygon

AMETA is a ERC-20 token build on the POLYGON blockchain, which ensures high transaction processing performance, low network fees, and high reliability

Rewards for Staking

Staking means that holders choose to stake their AMETA tokens, which are locked in a smart contract to earn income and cannot be used during the staking period.

Company Reserve

Every quarter, AMETAVERSE will use 20% of the profit to exchange $AMETA for BITCOIN to be used as a company reserve to consolidate the AMETAVERSE asset structure.

The AMETAVERSE game offers two ways to play: Single-player mode is free, while multiplayer mode requires players to place a corresponding amount of $AMETA bets at the beginning of each game.
