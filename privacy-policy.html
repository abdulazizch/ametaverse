Privacy Policy

1. PRIVACY POLICY
The following document governs and details the Privacy Policy of Ametaverse (https://www.ametaverse.io/) which is the Polygon-based platform through which we are offering our services. Subject to applicable regulations and rules the privacy of all of our users, customers, clients and buyers is protected and respected. This privacy statement is drafted in order to declare our website’s data protection and data collection practices by explaining specifically how we protect users’ data, and what measures have been taken in order to be in compliance with different local and global privacy laws. This policy is written for all the users, buyers and customers (herein collectively will be called “users”) to understand how our website (“we”, “our”, “Ametaverse”, “Ameta Token”) uses, collects, shares, and protects users’ information and data. We request our users to read this privacy policy statement before joining and using our services. The users of our services will be assumed to be in agreement with our privacy statement after we have published this Privacy Policy on and/or through our website.

2. COLLECTION OF DATA
Our website collects information that is necessary for the purpose of delivering our services. Personal information includes (but is not limited to) the name, e-mail address and location of the user. The information which will be collected in this process will be used to provide services to the user as well as in order to communicate with that user through e-mail and/or newsletters. Some information on our website/platform will be gathered automatically through different tools and plugins.
2.1. Users Information Collection.
There will be a collection of information from all of our users when they create an account on Ametaverse’s website if they will use any of our given services. The information that will be provided to us includes but is not limited to:
a. Email address
b. Name
c. Location
d. Users' Third-Party Related Information (Collected Automatically)
e. Payment Information (Processed Automatically)
f. Any additional information relating to the users that they provide us directly through our website or indirectly through our platform or online presence such as ‘cookies’.
2.2. Automatic Information Collection
We also collect information that is collected by general websites for the purpose of rendering normal services. This type of information includes (but is not limited to) Ametaverse services usage by users, referring nodes, device OS and its type, language and its preference, and data and time of users’ browsing history. We may release such information in aggregate form for the purpose of making our services better. Please note that in aggregate form none of the individual users can be identified. Our services may also collect information that is considered personal identifiable information such as IP addresses and other information collected through cookies.

3. USE OF COOKIES
Please read our Cookie Policy.

We rely on third-party platforms in order to render our services. Please note that all of these services are changeable and we may use other alternatives in case we find new, better, and improved third-party platforms. 5. PROTECTION AND SHARING OF CONSUMERS AND USERS' DATA
We are committed to protecting the personal data of consumers and users to the best of our ability. Data security is necessary and therefore in compliance with the global data privacy laws we have implemented measures in order to make sure that all data belonging to the consumers and users of our website is well protected. Our website neither collects any payment-related information nor do we control it and we only rely on blockchain-based transactions which are cannot be reversed.
We May Share Users’ Information In Following Instances:
a. When we are required by a court of law or legal authority to do so.
b. When our business is transferred or sold to a new company or owner and in that transaction, the data stored by us will also be transferred to the new owner.
c. We may store and transfer the personal data of the users out of their residential countries including out of the European Union regions in order to store and process our data. This step will be taken in order to make our services better. Users of our website hereby acknowledge that their data can be stored on hosting servers that are outside their residential jurisdiction.
d. We will share the users’ data with our service providers (such as web-hosting providers, payment partners and agents, etc.), affiliates, marketing partners, business partners, and others who have legal rights to access users’ data.

In compliance with the GDPR, this statement has been provided for all the users of our website who are using any or all of our services. All users have the right to access their personal information and they have the right to know how their data is being stored on our website. Users can also have their data removed or transferred from our website.
Users have the following rights.
a. The right to access.
b. The right to rectification.
c. The right to erasure
d. The right to restrict processing.

If any user makes a request, we have one month to respond to the user. If anyone would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at this email: support@ametaverse.io.

All information users provide to us is stored securely and treated with care and respect. If we do send users’ data outside the EEA, we will take reasonable steps to ensure that the recipient implements appropriate measures to protect users’ information.

8. CCPA COMPLIANCE
The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is an act enacted for the residents of the USA and specifically for the residents of the state of California. This law provides additional protections and rights for the protection of personal information for California residents. Our website does not sell personal information for monetary consideration; however, we do work with and share personal information with certain partners and third parties unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy. Under CCPA, users hold certain rights that apply to the collection, storing, and sharing of their personal information. Users can contact us to exercise their rights under CCPA by emailing us at the address below.
Under CCPA users have the following rights.
a. Users have the right to know what personal information our website collects.
b. Users have the right to know who we share their data with.
c. Users have the right to delete their data.
d. Users have the right to receive Non-Discrimination services.
8.1. PRIVACY OF CHILDREN AND PARENTS ACCOUNTS IN COMPLIANCE WITH COPPA
COPPA means The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act. This act was enacted in 1998 and under this act, all the measures have been made to protect the information of children, specifically children under 13. The main focus of the COPPA act is to put parents in control of the information that their children may be having access to. Our website does not knowingly collect information from children who are under 13 years of age. And we do not knowingly collect, store, maintain, or use personally identifiable information from kids or children under age 13. If you are under 13, you should never provide us with any personal information, nor should you be using our website and our services.
